Site Plan: U=U Website

Site Name

U=U: Undetectable = Untransmittable

This name directly communicates the core message of HIV treatment success and prevention. It uses the widely recognized medical equation format to emphasize the scientific basis of the concept.

Site Purpose

The site serves as an educational platform to:

  • Inform about the U=U concept and its significance in HIV treatment
  • Provide resources and support for people living with HIV
  • Combat stigma through education and empowerment
  • Connect visitors with additional resources and support services

Target Audience Scenarios

  • "As someone recently diagnosed with HIV, what does U=U mean for my future relationships?"
  • "How can I explain U=U to my partner and family members?"
  • "Where can I find scientific evidence supporting the U=U concept?"

Color Schema

Primary Color (Black)
Secondary Color (Red)
Accent Color (Light Pink)
Background Color
(White - Light Mode)

Usage:

  • Primary Color (#000000): Navigation background, text, borders
  • Secondary Color (#f44336): Accents, borders, headings, emphasis
  • Accent Color (#ffdddd): Background for info sections, encouragement section
  • Text Colors: White (#ffffff) on dark backgrounds, Gray (#808080) for secondary text
  • Gradient: Linear gradient with red and white (rgba(255, 0, 0, 0.8), rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.7))

Typography

  • Primary Font: Inter - Used for body text and general content
  • Secondary Font: Montserrat - Used for headings and emphasis
  • Accent Font: Bigshot One - Used for the logo and special elements
